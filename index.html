Famous Places to Visit in Spiti Valley

These are some of the must-see locations around Rangrik and nearby villages.

Key Monastery

Key Monastery (Kee/Key Gompa) is an 11th-century Tibetan Buddhist monastery built on top of a hill. Monks live and study here. You can sit inside the prayer hall, talk to the lamas, and see an unreal view of the entire Spiti Valley from above.

  • Historic Tibetan monastery
  • Peaceful meditation halls
  • Panoramic valley views

Chandratal Lake

Chandratal, also called the Moon Lake, is one of the most famous high-altitude lakes in the Himalayas. The water is crystal clear and changes color with the sun. People camp near the lake in summer and watch the stars at night.

  • High-altitude alpine lake
  • Camping and stargazing
  • Insane sunrise/sunset colors

Kibber Village

Kibber is one of the highest motorable villages in the world. Stone houses, clean air, absolute silence. In winter, this area is famous for snow leopard spotting safaris and beautiful white landscapes.

  • Traditional Spiti lifestyle
  • Snow leopard spotting in winter
  • Very high altitude village

Chicham Bridge

Chicham Bridge is one of the highest suspension bridges in Asia. It connects the villages of Kibber and Chicham over a deep gorge. The view from the bridge is dramatic and perfect for photos.
